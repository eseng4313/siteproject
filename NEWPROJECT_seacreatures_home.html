Sea Mammals

A mammal is an animal that gives birth to a live young instead of a egg. Also mammals secrete milk to their youngs for food. All mammals have fur or hair and are warm blooded. There are no mammals that can breath under water.

Sea Plants

There are different types of plants that live in the sea. All sea plants can breathe under water.Sea plants can provide a home for many species under water like fish. Also sea plants are food for some species in the sea.

Fish

Fishes are an animal species that lives under water and have gills. They have fins and have different diets for different species of fish. Fishes are not mammals because they lay eggs.
